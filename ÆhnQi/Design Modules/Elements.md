# #Elements

## Temperature Scale
1. Void
2. Ice
3. Water
4. Earth
5. Metal
6. Magma
7. Crystal
8. Air
9. Fire
10. Lightning
11. Chaos

## Combinations
- Steam = Water + Fire
- Plant = Water + Earth
- Current = (Water | Steam) + Lightning

## Interactions
- Plant + Lightning → ExplosionFX, FireDmg
- Plant + Ice + Death → Shatter
- Steam + Lightning → Current, AoE
- Ice + Fire → Water
- Metal + Lightning → Current
- Magma + Water → Earth
- Ice + Magma → Steam
