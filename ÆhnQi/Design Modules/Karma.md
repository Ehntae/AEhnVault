# #Karma

## Tracking
- Linked to SteamID
- Tracks:
  - Monsters Banished
  - Cores Restored
  - Deaths
  - Deeds

## Effects
- Influences initial genetics
- Prevents complete restart on death
- Modifies new cultivator starting stats

## Example
```
Player: Ehntae
Karma: 588,672
    [Darkness Cleared]
         Monsters Banished: 574,926
         Cores Restored: 14
         Deaths: 18
```
