# Alchemy

## DNA Mechanics
- Resources have DNA:
  - Mutagen
  - Graft
  - Herbs/plants
- Random plant gene generation

## Crafting
- Combines GenomeA + GenomeB
- DNA determines outcome
- Mixed genetics affect product

## Effects
```
Ailment Chances (DNA-based):
- Bleed: +1% -2%
- Ignite: -0.0032%
- Freeze: +13.01%

Stats Example:
- Movespeed: +8%
```
